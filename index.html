How To Play

Guess the wordle in 5, 6, or 7 tries, depending on the gamemode.

  • Each guess must be of either 4, 5, or 6 letters depending on the chosen game mode.
  • When pressing submit or enter, the game tiles will change colour to indicate how close you were to getting the word.

Game modes

There are two game modes: daily and practise. You must complete the daily mode for each word length before the practise mode unlocks for that word length.

You can turn on hard mode in the settings. When enabled, you MUST use any revealed letters in subsequent guesses.
